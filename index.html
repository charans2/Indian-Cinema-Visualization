Indian Cinema Visualization

Organized Chaos presents a visualization of the star-centric system in Indian popular cinema. The great majority of Bollywood stars are connected via family networks, formed over several decades. The lack of regulations in the film industry made it a lucrative opportunity for a fast return of capital during WWII. The studio system was replaced by a star-centric system.
